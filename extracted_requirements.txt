Top page main visual                                          Background discussion              Gem Life As Art       【The underlying concept】           "Life as art" means intentionally living with creativity, purpose, and self-expression, treating one’s experiences like an artist creates a masterpiece—making choices, finding beauty, problem-solving, and expressing your unique self in everyday actions, from cooking to career. It's about moving beyond routine to find meaning and applying artistic principles like imagination, adjusting to challenges, and creating a life that reflects your inner values and passions.           Gem Life as art incorporates gems as medium to engage your creative mind into living and subjectively creates aesthetic and meaningful experience.           The star is Life, not Gems.       【Design of Gemlife.world           based           on           the concept of Gem           Life           As           Art】           Conceptually, Gem Lifer is an artist, and RURI will work as a curator of the Museum of Gemlife Art (as well as an organizer/ administrator of gem lifers‘ community).           RURI will treat experiences, stories of Gem Lifer as art works potentially.
